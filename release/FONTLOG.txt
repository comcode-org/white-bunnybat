Basic Font Information

white-bunnybat is a font for the game Hackmud (https://www.hackmud.com/)
produced by ComCODE (https://comcode.org/)

IMPORTANT: white-bunnybat is derived from White Rabbit, which is NOT licensed
under OFL; any distribution or derivative of white-bunnybat must include
Matthew Welch's original copyright notice and MIT-style license, as in
LICENSE_MatthewWelch-MIT.txt, in addition to the OFL license here.
